Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Boost Solution Processing LLC collects, uses, stores, and protects information submitted through our website, contact forms, and website chat widget.

Last updated March 28, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to visitors of boostprocessing.com and to communications with Boost Solution Processing LLC regarding merchant processing, payment technology, terminal equipment, point-of-sale systems, customer support, and related services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Depending on how you contact us, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, business name, processing volume, preferred contact time, and message details.

Technical Information

We may collect browser, device, IP address, referral source, and basic usage information to help operate, secure, and improve the website.

3. How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested information about our services.
  • To schedule follow-up conversations, consultations, demos, or support interactions.
  • To operate, monitor, secure, and improve our website and communications.
  • To maintain business records and comply with legal, regulatory, or contractual obligations.

4. Website Forms, Chat Widget, and SMS

Our website may offer a standard contact form and a website chat widget. Information submitted through these tools is used to communicate with you about your request and our services.

If you choose to opt in to text messaging through our website chat widget or another approved method, message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out of SMS messages and HELP for assistance. SMS consent is not a condition of purchase.

5. Sharing of Information

We may share information with service providers that help us operate our website, communications, customer support, hosting, and related business systems. We do not use mobile opt-in data for unrelated third-party marketing.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use case categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

6. Cookies and Website Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to support site performance, security, and basic analytics. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

7. Data Retention and Security

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for legitimate business purposes, customer support, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and recordkeeping. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to help protect personal information.

8. Your Choices

  • You may request to update or correct your information.
  • You may ask us to stop marketing communications at any time.
  • You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP.
  • You may contact us to request information about data access or deletion, subject to applicable law.
